Baked Mac & Cheese

Description

What needs to be said? The perfect mac and cheese baked with a crispy, buttery crumb topping ideal for any gathering or just an easy dinner for the family.

Ingredients

  • 16oz Dry Macaroni
  • 1/3 Cup Salted Butter
  • 1/3 Cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 Teaspoon Onion Powder
  • 2 3/4 Cups Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Half & Half
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Dry Mustard Powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Seasoned Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 14oz Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 1/2oz Fresh Shredded Parmesan Cheese
    • Topping
  • 1/4 Cup Panko Bread Crumbs
  • 2 Teaspoons Melted Butter
  • 2oz Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 Tablespoons Shredded Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. In a small bowl, combine Panko bread crumbs, butter, cheddar cheese, and parmesan cheese and mix well. Set aside.
  3. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the macaroni and cook according to the package directions until al dente. Drain well, run under cold water, and set aside.
  4. Meanwhile, melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour, onion powder, and garlic powder. Cook for 2 minutes while stirring.
  5. Gradually add the milk and cream, whisking until smooth after each addition. Continue cooking over medium heat until the mixture comes to a simmer and let simmer for 1 minute.
  6. Remove the sauce from the heat and add cheddar cheese, parmesan cheese, and seasonings. Whisk until melted and smooth.
  7. Toss the sauce with the drained pasta and gently spoon it into a 9×13 casserole dish.
  8. Sprinkle the crumb mixture over top and bake for 20 to 22 minutes or until bubbly and topping is browned. Do not overcook.
